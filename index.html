Welcome


Are you interested in creating art? Look no further! We are an arts club located in Älta Centrum. Here you can explore and try out different ways of painting and drawing to sculpting and pottery.


Arts in Älta is an arts club for members only. By joining the club you get access to our studio and art supplies. The studio is equipped with all the tools and necessities needed to make your creativity and vision come to life. You’ll meet and get to know other creative minds and have a fun time together.

For 200 SEK/ month we offer you:

  • Art Supplies
  • Tools Needed
  • Storage for created objects
  • Some Refreshment
